Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: This site sets no cookies, collects no personal data, and sends no information to third-party advertising or tracking services. Fonts are served from this site's own files. If optional analytics are enabled, they use a privacy-first service that does not identify individuals.

Who we are

This website is the Longevity & Wellness Lecture Library, operated by Ricky Chang. It is a personal educational publishing project with no commercial purpose.

Data we collect

We collect no personal information. There are no user accounts, no login, no contact forms, and no comment sections. Visiting this site does not require you to provide any information about yourself.

Cookies

This site sets no cookies of any kind — functional, analytical, or advertising.

Fonts

All typefaces used on this site (Newsreader, Manrope, and Fragment Mono) are served directly from this site's own files. No requests are made to Google Fonts or any external typography service, so no browsing data is transmitted to third parties for the purpose of loading fonts.

Analytics

This site may use Plausible Analytics, a European-hosted, privacy-first analytics service. If enabled, Plausible collects only aggregate, non-personal statistics such as page views and referrer domains. It does not use cookies, does not fingerprint browsers, and cannot identify individual visitors. No personal data is ever stored or transmitted. For details, see Plausible's privacy policy.

If analytics are not currently active on this site, no usage data is collected at all.

External links

Lectures link to academic journals and research papers hosted on external websites. Those sites operate under their own privacy policies, which we do not control. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Content accuracy and medical disclaimer

Lectures are researched and drafted with the assistance of AI language models and are intended to reflect scientific understanding at the time of writing. The field continues to evolve; individual lectures may not reflect the most current research.

Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendation. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making any health-related decisions.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, you may reach us through the contact information provided in the lecture series.
